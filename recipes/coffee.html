Coffee

Description

Coffee. That morning beverage that brings so many of us to life. It is made from grinding the roasted seeds of the Coffee fruit tree.

Ingredients

  • Keurig Coffee Machine
  • Keurig Cup or any Keurig-matching cup
  • Water

Steps

  1. Grab a keurig cup.
  2. Put it in the machine.
  3. Put water in the machine.
  4. Put mug where coffee will dispense.
  5. Hit brew.
  6. If machine does not brew, make sure you turned it on.
  7. Take coffee and begin day.
