Oatmeal

Coffee

Description

Oatmeal. Some people love it. It can taste alright if enough stuff is added to it. Which I rarely do. So I rarely eat it.

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Maple Syrup or Honey

Steps

  1. Add oatmeal to a pot.
  2. Add water to the pot.
  3. If desired, add butter for some extra flavor and mouthfeel.
  4. Cook oatmeal until it is done to your liking.
