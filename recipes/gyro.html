Chicken Gyro

Description

Ingredients

  • Pita Wrap
  • Chicken
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Olive Oil
  • Lemon
  • Tomatoes
  • Feta Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook chicken. For simplicity, I like to bake chicken that I've brushed with olive oil and seasoned.
  2. While chicken cooks, make the tzatziki sauce from greek yogurt, olive oil and fresh lemon juice. Proportion by taste.
  3. Warm pita on a comal or similar stovetop appliance, or in an oven.
  4. Place pita on a plate. Spread tzatziki sauce as a base on pita and top with chicken. Add diced tomatoes and feta cheese if desired. Wrap pita around chicken and enjoy.
